How do I change the email I use for my account?

Mobirise
If you don't have any paid extensions you could just register with new email for one more time.
If you purchased some extensions, all of them are attached to the email, which was mentioned during the activation.
It's impossible to reattach keys to another email or activate them twice.
In the case, if you purchased them not very long time ago (usually during the month), we could send you the new keys to extensions for one more time, so you could activate them with the proper email.
The old (activated) key will be blocked.
